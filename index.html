ACHIEVEMENTS

The Joint Professional Collegiate Event Award is jointly recognises a joint event by a professional SWE Group and a Collegiate SWE Group. This award is a recognition of the work done by the affiliate for the Incube conference held in 2019 in association with Cummins India Limited.

The Outstanding Outreach Event Award recognizes a group or groups who plan and carry out a high quality outreach event to inspire future engineers. This award is a recognition of the work done by the affiliate for awareness of STEM at Maher Ashram Wakad, a NGO Idea Foundation and MKSSS's Mahilashram School.
